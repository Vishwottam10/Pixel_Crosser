THE RULES (ALL TWO OF THEM)

No tutorials, no onboarding wizard. Pixel Crosser hands you the controls and gets out of the way.

RULE 01

Nudge a lane at a time

Every tap or key press moves you exactly one square — up, down, left or right. Precise, deliberate, no accidental sprints into traffic.

RULE 02

Don't get flattened

Traffic keeps rolling whether you move or not. Time your crossing, breathe, and make it to the top row in one piece.

WHY IT'S CHILL

Casual doesn't mean lazy. Every lane is hand-tuned to feel fair, even when it's laughing at you.

One thumb, zero stress

Pick it up mid-queue, put it down when your bus arrives. No save-scumming required.

Hand-placed pixels

Every lane, car and blade of grass is drawn square by square — no stock asset packs.

Bite-sized runs

Twelve rows stand between you and the goal. Most runs are over before your coffee cools.

Encrypted, not sold

Traffic in transit is encrypted, and nothing you do gets handed to third parties.

A LOOK INSIDE
